About Our Chapter

IEEE Computer Society Student Chapter at Ramco Institute of Technology brings together passionate students dedicated to advancing technology. We organize workshops, hackathons, and industry talks focusing on AI, Computer Vision, and assistive technologies.

IEEE Membership Benefits

  • Access to IEEE conferences, journals, and technical resources
  • Networking with industry professionals and global peers
  • Scholarships, travel grants, and career development programs
  • Latest updates on cutting-edge tech events worldwide

About Ramco Institute of Technology

Located in Rajapalayam, Tamil Nadu, RIT is a premier engineering institution affiliated with Anna University, specializing in Computer Science, AI, and emerging technologies with state-of-the-art labs and industry partnerships.
